Operations Manager

Company Overview

We are a small, fast-growing company based out of Del Mar, CA. Our team consists of four successful entrepreneurs in our 20s. Our new company is in the self-improvement space.

Job Description:

We're hiring an Operations Manager to handle day-to-day office tasks, assist with content creation, and help maintain a productive environment. This role will include a mix of filming, managing documents, cooking, and cleaning.

We work out of a beautiful house in Del Mar. You'll get to spend time hanging out by the pool and going on hikes during work hours every week.

Key Responsibilities

  • Film content when needed and transfer videos to editors
  • Manage documents and handle basic admin tasks
  • Prepare one meal per day
  • Clean and organize the office
  • Assist with additional tasks as needed

Qualifications

  • Fast learner, organized, and diligent
  • Strong social skills
  • Able to commute to Del Mar, CA

Compensation and Hours

  • $3,000-$4,000 per month
  • 20-25 hours per week - very flexible
  • Opportunity for future full-time employment
